Our Products

DiGestoChar for Dairy Cows

An innovative animal feed designed to revolutionize dairy farming. Each 25g/day serving significantly reduces methane emissions while boosting milk production by an additional 1.4 liters per day.

Key Benefits:

  • 18% reduction in methane emissions
  • 1.4L additional milk production daily
  • Enhanced digestibility in rumen
  • Reduced calving intervals
  • Better utilization of grass feed

DiGestoChar for Egg-Laying Hens

Designed to improve digestion in egg-laying hens, leading to healthier birds and enhanced weight gain while reducing feeding frequency and costs.

Key Benefits:

  • Improved digestion and feed efficiency
  • Cleaner eggs and better quality
  • Reduced bacterial enteritis
  • Lower ammonia emissions
  • Stronger eggshells

DiGestoChar for Broilers

Significantly enhances digestion in broilers, promoting healthier birds and accelerating weight gain while reducing feed costs.

Key Benefits:

  • Accelerated weight gain
  • Reduced feed costs
  • Better protein synthesis
  • Improved mineral absorption
  • Enhanced meat quality

DiGestoChar for Fish Farming

Instrumental in improving breeding conditions by significantly reducing antibiotic needs while enhancing water quality and fish growth.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduced antibiotic dependency
  • Improved water quality
  • Faster fish growth
  • Enhanced digestion
  • Better disease resistance

Biodigester Enhancement

Our biochar products significantly enhance biodigester efficiency by optimizing organic material breakdown and increasing methane yield.

Key Benefits:

  • Increased methane yield
  • Stabilized digestion process
  • Reduced harmful by-products
  • More efficient energy conversion
  • Cleaner operations
